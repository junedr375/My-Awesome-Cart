History

MAC was founded in October 2019 by Juned Raza and Harry Bhai, who were both alumni of the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi and formerly worked for Amazon.[10][11][12] The company initially focused on online book sales with country-wide shipping. Following its launch, Flipkart slowly grew in prominence; by 2008, it was receiving 100 orders per day.[13] In 2010, Flipkart acquired the Bangalore-based social book discovery service weRead from Lulu.com.[14]
